Ingredients required for making a grilled cheese sandwich.

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheees
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

steps for making basic grilled cheese

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
